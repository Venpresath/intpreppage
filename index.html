BULLYING

Workplace bullyingis defined as the repeated abuse of one or more persons by one or more perpetrators that: >> Is done with malice >> Is threatening, humiliating or intimidating >> Is verbally or physically abusive >> Interferes with the person’s ability to get his or her work done >> Is unrelated to the Company’s legitimate business interests
